Privacy

Privacy policy written for a real service business, not a boilerplate dump.

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains what information China Partner Hub collects, how it is used, and how sourcing-related information is handled.

1. Information we collect

When you contact us through the website, we collect the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, business name, product category, supplier details, and message content. If you contact us directly by email or WhatsApp, we receive the information you send through those channels.

2. How we use the information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, review supplier-verification requests, discuss sourcing projects, and deliver the services you ask about. We do not sell or rent your personal information. Product details, supplier names, and sourcing context are treated as confidential business information.

3. Storage and processing

Website form submissions are processed through Formspree and delivered to our email. We do not currently operate a custom website database for form storage. Information is retained for as long as reasonably necessary to handle the inquiry or the related business relationship.

4. Cookies and analytics

This website is not designed around invasive tracking. If hosting, CDN, or form-processing tools collect technical data for delivery, uptime, or security, that processing is governed by their own policies.

5. Third-party tools

We may rely on third-party services such as Formspree, email providers, messaging apps, and site delivery infrastructure to run the business. Those providers may process data according to their own terms and privacy rules.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at info@chinapartnerhub.com.
