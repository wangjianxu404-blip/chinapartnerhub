Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026

1. Services

ChinaPartnerHub provides China sourcing services including factory search, supplier verification, production management, quality inspection, and shipping coordination. Service details and pricing are outlined on our Services page and in individual client agreements.

2. Pricing & Payment

Our commission rates are transparent: 8% for full-service sourcing, 5% for production management only, and free for basic supplier verification. Minimum fee of $100 applies. Clients pay factories directly; our commission is invoiced separately. We recommend staged payments (30/40/30) via PayPal, Wise, Alipay, or bank transfer.

3. No Factory Price Markup

We do not mark up factory prices. Clients see the exact factory quote and pay the factory directly. Our commission is transparent and separate from the product cost.

4. Limitation of Liability

We act as a sourcing partner and advisor. While we exercise due diligence in factory verification and production management, we are not the manufacturer. The factory is responsible for product quality and delivery. Our liability is limited to the amount of our service fee.

5. Client Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for providing accurate product specifications, timely approvals, and payment according to the agreed schedule. Delays caused by client actions may affect production timelines.

6. Confidentiality

We treat all client information, product designs, and supplier relationships as confidential. We do not share client-specific information with other clients or third parties without permission.

7. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes will first be addressed through direct communication. If unresolved, disputes shall be handled under the laws of the People's Republic of China.
