The Process

How we take your product from idea to delivery

Five steps. No black boxes. At every stage, you know exactly what's happening on the ground in China.

1

You tell us what you need

Send us your product details — a photo, a sketch, a link, or just a description. Tell us your target quantity, budget range, and timeline. The more specific you are, the faster we move.

What we need from you:

✓ Product description or reference
✓ Target quantity and budget
✓ Any specific requirements (materials, certifications, packaging)
✓ Timeline expectations
2

We find the right factories

We search our network of vetted factories and industry contacts — not just Alibaba. We shortlist 3–5 factories that actually match your product, quantity, and budget. No trading companies pretending to be manufacturers.

Our search covers:

✓ Our existing verified factory network
✓ Industry contacts and referrals
✓ Factory visits to new candidates
✓ Cross-reference with export records
3

We verify in person

We visit the factory floor, check the production line, verify samples locally, and compare suppliers side by side. You get a clear recommendation with real data — not sales pitches.

What we check:

✓ Business license & registration
✓ Factory floor vs office address
✓ Equipment & production capacity
✓ Sample quality & material verification
4

We manage production

Once you approve a factory, we manage the entire production process. We track progress, run spot-checks, send real photos and videos from the factory floor, and solve problems before they become disasters.

You get:

✓ Real-time production updates via WhatsApp
✓ Photos and videos from the factory floor
✓ Quality spot-checks during production
✓ Problem resolution — we fix it, not you
5

We deliver to your door

Final quality inspection, shipping coordination, customs documentation — we handle it all. Your goods arrive at your door while you focus on growing your business.

We handle:

✓ Pre-shipment quality inspection
✓ Shipping method selection & booking
✓ Customs documentation
✓ Tracking until goods arrive
